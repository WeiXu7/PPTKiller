## Imaging Findings

- Location: Left lower lobe.
- Size: Approximately 1.3 cm on initial CT.
- Morphology: Irregular small nodule with surrounding patchy opacity.
- Boundary: Blurred margin.
- Density: Solid inflammatory-appearing opacity.
- Interval change: Marked absorption on follow-up after anti-inflammatory treatment.

## Final Diagnosis And Evidence

- Final diagnosis: Inflammatory pulmonary nodule.
- Verification: Follow-up CT showed substantial absorption after treatment.
- Pathology: Not performed.
